Privacy Notice

Effective date: June 2026  ·  LimeLogic Media Lab, Trinidad & Tobago

Straight talk: we collect only what we need to do good work with you. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. Here's exactly what happens when you reach out.

What we collect

When you submit the contact form, we receive:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your company or project name (if provided)
  • Your message — what you're building, what good looks like

That's it. We don't run analytics, don't use tracking pixels, and don't set any non-essential cookies.

How we use it

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Scope and quote your project
  • Follow up if you've expressed interest in working together

We won't add you to a mailing list or send you marketing without asking first.

Who processes it

Contact form submissions are handled by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form processor. Formspree stores your submission and delivers it to our inbox. Their privacy policy is at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We then retain your enquiry details in our own inbox and project management tools for up to 12 months, after which they are deleted unless you've become a client.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete any information we hold about you at any time. Just email us at admin@limelogicstudio.com and we'll sort it within 7 days.

Cookies

This site doesn't use cookies. No banners needed — there's nothing to accept.

Changes to this notice

If we change how we handle your data, we'll update this page and the effective date above. Nothing material will change without notice.

Contact

Questions? Email admin@limelogicstudio.com.
