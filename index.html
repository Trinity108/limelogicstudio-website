Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your discovery call.

What happens in the discovery call?

We'll discuss your campaign goals, target audience, brand guidelines, and deliverable needs. If it's a good fit, we'll walk you through timeline, pricing, and next steps. No pressure, no obligation—just clarity.

When does the 7-day timeline start?

Timeline starts after we receive your creative brief, brand assets, and 50% deposit. We'll send a kickoff checklist so you know exactly what we need upfront.

What do you need from me to get started?

We'll need: (1) Creative brief or campaign overview, (2) Brand assets (logos, colors, fonts), (3) Visual references or mood boards, (4) Deadline and delivery format preferences. We'll guide you through it step by step.

What formats do you deliver?

All major social formats: MP4 (1080x1920, 1080x1080, 1920x1080), JPG/PNG for static, organized by platform. You'll get full source files plus usage rights.

How do revisions work?

All packages include 2 rounds of revisions. We'll send a concept preview in 48 hours—you approve direction before we scale. Major scope changes may require a small add-on fee.

What's your typical budget range?

Projects range from TTD $4,500 to $20,000+ depending on scope. Starter packages start at TTD $4,500. Core campaigns (most popular) run TTD $8,000–$15,000. Premium or retainer work is custom-quoted.

Can you do rush delivery?

Yes! Rush delivery (3–5 days) is available with a +25% rush fee and subject to current availability. Contact us ASAP if you have an urgent deadline.

Do you provide licensed music?

Yes! All video packages include commercially licensed music (no copyright strikes). We also offer custom voiceover and AI-generated music tailored to Caribbean vibes.
